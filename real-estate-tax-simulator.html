Important Disclaimer: Tax laws change frequently and vary based on individual circumstances. This information is for general guidance only. Always consult with a qualified tax advisor or real estate attorney in the country where you're purchasing property.

Tax Optimization Strategies:

  • Holding Company: Consider buying through a company for tax benefits (consult professional)
  • Primary Residence Exemption: Many countries offer capital gains exemption
  • 1031 Exchange (USA): Defer taxes by reinvesting in similar property
  • Depreciation: Deduct property depreciation against rental income (USA, Germany)
  • Mortgage Interest Deduction: Available in many countries for primary residence
  • Long-Term Holding: Reduced/exempt capital gains after 10+ years (Germany, Italy)
  • Rental Deductions: Maintenance, management fees, insurance, property tax
  • Special Regimes: NHR in Portugal, Beckham Law in Spain for expats
