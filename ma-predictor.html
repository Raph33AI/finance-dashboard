Data Filters Guide

How to use the M&A Predictor:

  1. Select your desired Time Period (1 week to 1 year)
  2. Choose the Max Filings to analyze (100 to 2,000)
  3. Click "Load M&A Data" to retrieve SEC filings
  4. Click "View Details" on any deal to see the full parsed document

What you'll see:

  • Recent M&A Deals: All S-4 and 8-K filings in your selected period
  • Parsed Documents: Clean, formatted SEC filing content
  • Company Information: CIK, filing date, document type
Note: Large datasets may take time to load. Start with shorter periods.
