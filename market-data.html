📈 Understanding Price Charts

📊 Technical Indicators

1️⃣ SMA (Simple Moving Average)

Average price over a period (e.g., 20 or 50 days). Helps identify trends.

  • Price above SMA: Bullish signal 📈
  • Price below SMA: Bearish signal 📉

2️⃣ EMA (Exponential Moving Average)

Like SMA but gives more weight to recent prices. More responsive to changes.

3️⃣ Bollinger Bands

Shows price volatility. Price touching upper band = overbought, lower band = oversold.

💡 Combine multiple indicators for better signals!

📊 RSI - Relative Strength Index

📐 What is RSI?

The RSI measures the speed and magnitude of price changes. It oscillates between 0 and 100.

📈 Interpretation

  • RSI > 70: Overbought → Possible reversal down ⚠️
  • RSI < 30: Oversold → Possible reversal up 🚀
  • RSI = 50: Neutral

Formula:

RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + RS)]

RS = Average Gain / Average Loss (over 14 periods)

💡 Trading Strategy: Buy when RSI crosses above 30, sell when it crosses below 70.

📊 MACD - Moving Average Convergence Divergence

📐 What is MACD?

The MACD shows the relationship between two moving averages. It consists of:

  • MACD Line: EMA(12) - EMA(26)
  • Signal Line: EMA(9) of MACD
  • Histogram: MACD - Signal

📈 Trading Signals

  • MACD crosses above Signal: Buy signal 🟢
  • MACD crosses below Signal: Sell signal 🔴
  • Histogram increasing: Momentum strengthening
💡 Pro Tip: MACD works best in trending markets, not in sideways/ranging markets.

🎯 Trading Signals Explained

🚦 Signal Types

🟢 Bullish Signals (Buy)

  • RSI oversold (<30) and reversing up
  • MACD crossing above signal line
  • Price bouncing off lower Bollinger Band
  • Golden Cross: Short MA crosses above Long MA

🔴 Bearish Signals (Sell)

  • RSI overbought (>70) and reversing down
  • MACD crossing below signal line
  • Price rejected at upper Bollinger Band
  • Death Cross: Short MA crosses below Long MA
⚠️ Warning: No signal is 100% accurate. Always combine multiple indicators and use risk management!
