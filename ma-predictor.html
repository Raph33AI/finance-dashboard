Data Filters Guide

How to use the M&A Predictor:

  1. Select your desired Time Period (1 week to 1 year)
  2. Choose the Max Filings to analyze (100 to 2,000)
  3. Click "Analyze M&A Deals" to retrieve SEC filings
  4. Review Alphy AI Recommendations for top merger candidates
  5. Click "View" on any deal to see the full parsed document

What you'll see:

  • AI Recommendations: Top 6 deals scored by probability analysis
  • Recent M&A Deals: All S-4 and 8-K filings in your selected period
  • AI Scores (0-100): Dynamic scoring based on 6+ factors (no random values)
  • Parsed Documents: Clean, formatted SEC filing content with offerings data

AI Scoring Factors:

  • Form Type Relevance: S-4 (100), 8-K Item 2.01 (90), Item 1.01 (80)
  • Filing Recency: Last 7 days (100), 14 days (90), 30 days (75)
  • Company Activity: Multiple filings indicate active M&A strategy
  • Keyword Signals: "merger", "acquisition", "takeover", etc.
  • Filing Complexity: Detailed filings suggest significant transactions
  • 8-K Item Relevance: Material agreements and acquisition completions
Note: Large datasets may take 30-60 seconds to load. Start with shorter periods for faster results.
