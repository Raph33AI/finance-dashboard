1. Account Setup

Creating your AlphaVault AI account is quick and secure.

1

Create Your Account

Visit alphavault-ai.com/signup and register using:

  • Google OAuth (single sign-on)
  • Microsoft OAuth (enterprise accounts)
2

Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from no-reply@alphavault-ai.com. Click the link to activate your account.

3. Your First Analysis

Let's analyze your first stock using AI-powered tools.

Step-by-Step Guide
  1. Navigate to Stocks > Advanced Analysis
  2. Enter a stock symbol (e.g., AAPL, TSLA, NVDA)
  3. Click "Analyze"
  4. Wait 5-10 seconds for AI processing
  5. Review the multi-tab analysis:
    • Overview: Key metrics, P/E ratio, market cap
    • Fundamentals: Revenue, EPS, margins
    • Technical: RSI, MACD, support/resistance
    • AI Prediction: Price targets with confidence scores

Pro Tip: Use the "Compare" feature to benchmark against competitors in the same sector.

4. Create Your Portfolio

Build and track your investment portfolio with advanced analytics.

1

Add Your First Position

Go to Budget > Dashboard Budget and click "Add Position".

  • Enter symbol (e.g., MSFT)
  • Quantity (number of shares)
  • Purchase price
  • Date acquired
2

Monitor Performance

Your portfolio dashboard automatically calculates:

  • Total value (updated in real-time)
  • Unrealized gains/losses
  • Sector allocation pie chart
  • Risk metrics (Sharpe ratio, beta, volatility)
3

Optimize with AI

Use Simulation > Markowitz to optimize your allocation for maximum Sharpe ratio.

The optimizer considers historical correlations and volatility to suggest the ideal weight for each asset.
