AI Probability Scoring

6 Factors, 1 Powerful M&A Score

Our AI engine analyzes SEC filings using 6 weighted components to generate 0-100 M&A probability scores

Form Type Relevance

30% Weight

Prioritizes S-4 merger filings (100 pts) and 8-K material events (50-90 pts based on item codes: 2.01 completion, 1.01 agreements, 5.02 leadership changes).

Filing Recency

20% Weight

Gives higher scores to recent filings: 100 pts for last 7 days, 90 pts for 14 days, 75 pts for 30 days. Older filings score progressively lower.

Company Activity Level

15% Weight

Detects unusual activity patterns. Companies with 5+ filings in the period score 95 pts, suggesting active M&A processes or multiple deal stages.

Filing Detail Level

15% Weight

Analyzes document length and complexity. Longer filings (500+ chars) score up to 90 pts, indicating comprehensive merger agreements or material details.

M&A Keyword Signals

15% Weight

Scans for 15+ M&A keywords: "merger", "acquisition", "definitive agreement", "tender offer", "takeover", "strategic investment". High-value keywords score 12 pts each.

8-K Item Relevance

5% Weight

Specific to 8-K filings: Item 2.01 (completion) scores 100 pts, Item 1.01 (material agreement) 90 pts, Item 5.02 (leadership changes) 70 pts.
