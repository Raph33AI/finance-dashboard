Security at AlphaVault AI

Your data security is our top priority

Enterprise-Grade Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your financial data and ensure platform integrity.

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted using industry-standard 256-bit SSL/TLS in transit and AES-256 at rest, ensuring your information remains secure.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Optional 2FA/MFA using authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) or SMS codes for enhanced account security.

Secure Infrastructure

Hosted on AWS with SOC 2 Type II compliance, redundant backups, and 99.9% uptime SLA.

24/7 Monitoring

Real-time threat detection, intrusion prevention systems, and automated security alerts to protect against attacks.

Penetration Testing

Regular security audits and penetration tests by third-party experts to identify and fix vulnerabilities.

Data Backups

Automated daily backups with 30-day retention, geo-redundant storage, and disaster recovery plans.

Compliance

GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 compliant. We adhere to strict data protection regulations.

Employee Training

All employees undergo security training and background checks. Access is granted on a need-to-know basis.

Privacy by Design

Data minimization, anonymization, and privacy-first architecture. We never sell your data.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us immediately at support@alphavault.ai. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond within 24 hours.

Bug Bounty Program: We offer rewards for critical vulnerabilities. Contact us for more details.
