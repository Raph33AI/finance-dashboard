Core Concept

What is Risk Parity?

A systematic approach to portfolio construction that equalizes risk contributions instead of capital allocations

Key Difference

Traditional Allocation Problem

Classic 60/40 portfolios allocate equal capital, but high-volatility assets (like stocks at 18% vol) dominate risk, contributing 90%+ of total portfolio risk.

  • Unbalanced risk exposure
  • Stock-driven volatility
  • Poor diversification

Risk Parity Solution

Risk Parity allocates capital to equalize risk contributions. Each asset contributes the same percentage to total portfolio risk, achieving true diversification.

  • Balanced risk across all assets
  • Enhanced diversification benefits
  • More stable performance

Inverse Volatility Weighting

Risk Parity uses inverse volatility weighting: assets with lower volatility receive higher capital allocations to equalize their risk contribution.

  • Weight ∝ 1 / Volatility
  • Accounts for correlations
  • Covariance matrix optimization

Institutional Adoption

Risk Parity is used by Bridgewater Associates (All Weather Fund), AQR Capital, and major pension funds managing $500B+ AUM.

  • Proven at institutional scale
  • All-weather performance
  • Crisis-tested strategy
