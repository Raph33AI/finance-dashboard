1. Login Issues

Cannot Login - "Invalid Credentials"

1

Verify Email Address

Ensure you're using the email address you registered with. Email is case-insensitive.

2

Reset Password

  • Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your inbox (and spam folder) for reset link
  • Link expires in 1 hour

Didn't receive email? Check spam folder or request a new link after 5 minutes.

3

Clear Browser Cache

Old cookies can cause login issues.

  • Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+Delete > Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+Delete > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

Two-Factor Authentication Issues

1

Code Not Working

Check these common causes:

  • Time sync: Ensure your device clock is accurate (authenticator apps use time-based codes)
  • Expired code: Codes refresh every 30 seconds, enter quickly
  • Typo: Codes are 6 digits, no spaces
2

Lost Access to Authenticator App

Use your backup codes (generated during 2FA setup).

No Backup Codes? Contact support with government-issued ID for manual verification.

2. Data Not Loading

Stock Data Shows "No Data Available"

1

Verify Symbol

Ensure the stock symbol is correct. Use official ticker (e.g., AAPL not Apple).

2

Check Market Hours

Market Trading Hours (EST)
US Markets 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM
Pre-Market 4:00 AM - 9:30 AM
After-Hours 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Outside these hours, data updates are delayed.

3

API Rate Limits

Free tier: 5 requests/minute. If exceeded, wait 60 seconds or upgrade to Pro.

Pro Users: 60 requests/minute with real-time data.

Charts Not Rendering

1

Disable Ad Blockers

Some ad blockers interfere with Chart.js. Whitelist alphavault-ai.com.

2

Enable JavaScript

Our platform requires JavaScript. Check browser settings to ensure it's enabled.

3. Performance Issues

Slow Loading Times

Check Internet Speed

Minimum 5 Mbps recommended. Test at fast.com.

Clear Cache

Old cached data can slow down loading.

Disable Extensions

Browser extensions can interfere with scripts.

Update Browser

Use the latest version for optimal performance.

Page Freezing or Crashing

1

Reduce Portfolio Size

Portfolios with 100+ positions may cause performance issues on older devices. Archive unused portfolios.

2

Close Other Tabs

Multiple heavy tabs consume RAM. Close unused tabs.

3

Hardware Acceleration

Enable in browser settings for better graphics performance.

Chrome: Enable Hardware Acceleration

Settings > System > Use hardware acceleration when available

4. Payment Problems

Payment Declined

1

Common Causes

  • Insufficient funds: Check account balance
  • Expired card: Update card details in Settings > Billing
  • Incorrect CVV: Re-enter 3-digit security code
  • Billing address mismatch: Must match card statement
  • International card: Some banks block international transactions
2

Contact Your Bank

Ask your bank to whitelist charges from "AlphaVault AI" or "Stripe".

Subscription Not Active After Payment

1

Wait 5 Minutes

Payment processing can take up to 5 minutes. Refresh the page.

2

Check Email

You should receive a confirmation email from billing@alphavault-ai.com.

3

Contact Support

If still not active after 10 minutes, email billing@alphavault-ai.com with:

  • Your account email
  • Payment confirmation screenshot
  • Last 4 digits of card used

Refund Requests

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If unsatisfied within 14 days of purchase, request a full refund at billing@alphavault-ai.com. Refunds processed within 5-7 business days.
