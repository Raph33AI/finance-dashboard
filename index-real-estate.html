Notary & Transfer Fees

Accurate calculation of notary fees, transfer taxes, stamp duty, and registration costs. France: 7.5%, UK: 1.5% + SDLT, USA: 3.5% closing costs, Spain: 8% ITP.

Property Tax Rates

Annual property tax calculations by country. France: 1.5%, UK: Council Tax (¬£2000/year), USA: 1.2%, Spain: 0.8% IBI, Portugal: 0.5% IMI.

Capital Gains Tax

Capital gains tax rules for property sales. France: 36.2%, UK: 28%, USA: 20% (+ state tax), Germany: 45% (exempt after 10 years), Italy: 26%.

Rental Income Tax

Rental income taxation by country. France: 30%, UK: 40% (with 20% mortgage relief), USA: 24% + depreciation, Spain: 21% Cedolare Secca (40% deduction).

Appreciation Rates

Annual property appreciation rates. France: 3%, UK: 4%, USA: 4.5%, Netherlands: 5.5%, Portugal: 5%, Germany: 3%, Switzerland: 2.5%.

Primary Residence Exemptions

Tax exemptions for primary residences. France: 100% CGT exempt, UK: 100% exempt, USA: $250k single/$500k married, Italy: IMU + CGT exempt (5+ years).

How It Works

Simulation Workflow

From property details to comprehensive financial analysis in 4 steps

1

Property Configuration

Select country and property type (Primary, Secondary, Rental). Enter purchase price, down payment, interest rate, loan duration, and monthly salary.

2

Financial Calculations

System calculates monthly mortgage payment, borrowing capacity, debt ratio, notary fees, property tax, and total acquisition costs using country-specific rules.

3

Results Analysis

View amortization schedule, wealth evolution chart (30 years), rental yield (if applicable), and comprehensive tax breakdown with Highcharts visualizations.

4

Save & Portfolio

Manually save simulation with custom name to Firestore. Simulation auto-adds to portfolio with equity tracking, value appreciation, and loan balance updates.
