What is the KG Score?

The Knowledge Graph (KG) Score is a confidence metric provided by Google's Knowledge Graph API. It represents how well-documented and verified an entity is within Google's vast knowledge base.

Score Interpretation

0-100
Low
100-500
Moderate
500-1000
High
1000+
Exceptional

Factors Influencing KG Score

  • Amount of verified information available
  • Number of authoritative sources (Wikipedia, official websites)
  • Entity prominence and global recognition
  • Data freshness and update frequency
  • Cross-reference validation across multiple databases
