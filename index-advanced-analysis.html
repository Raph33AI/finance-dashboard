14 Institutional-Grade Indicators

Professional trading indicators used by Wall Street analysts, now accessible to everyone

Most Used

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Identifies overbought (>70) and oversold (<30) conditions to detect optimal entry and exit points.

  • Overbought/Oversold zones detection
  • Divergence analysis for reversals
  • 14-period standard calculation

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

Tracks momentum changes through MACD line, signal line, and histogram crossovers.

  • Bullish/Bearish crossover signals
  • Histogram strength analysis
  • 12/26/9 standard parameters

Stochastic Oscillator

Compares closing price to price range over time to identify momentum shifts.

  • %K and %D line crossovers
  • 80/20 overbought/oversold levels
  • Fast and slow stochastic modes

Williams %R

Measures momentum on a -100 to 0 scale to detect extreme market conditions.

  • Deep oversold below -80
  • Deep overbought above -20
  • 14-period lookback window

ADX (Average Directional Index)

Measures trend strength (>25 = strong trend) and directional movement (+DI/-DI).

  • Trend strength quantification
  • Directional indicators (+DI/-DI)
  • Ranging vs trending market detection

OBV (On-Balance Volume)

Cumulative volume indicator to confirm price trends and detect divergences.

  • Volume confirmation of price moves
  • Bullish/Bearish divergence detection
  • Accumulation/Distribution signals

ATR (Average True Range)

Measures market volatility to set appropriate stop-loss levels and position sizing.

  • Volatility measurement
  • Optimal stop-loss calculation
  • Breakout potential detection

MFI (Money Flow Index)

Volume-weighted RSI to identify buying/selling pressure and money flow strength.

  • Money flow strength analysis
  • 80/20 overbought/oversold zones
  • Volume-price relationship

CCI (Commodity Channel Index)

Identifies cyclical trends and overbought/oversold conditions (±100 zones).

  • Cyclical pattern detection
  • ±100 breakout signals
  • Mean reversion opportunities

Ultimate Oscillator

Multi-timeframe oscillator (7/14/28 periods) for comprehensive momentum analysis.

  • Triple timeframe analysis
  • 70/30 overbought/oversold
  • Reduced false signals

ROC (Rate of Change)

Measures percentage price change over time to detect momentum acceleration.

  • Momentum acceleration tracking
  • Positive/negative momentum zones
  • Zero-line crossover signals

Aroon Indicator

Tracks time since highest high and lowest low to identify trend changes.

  • Trend change early detection
  • Aroon Up/Down crossovers
  • Consolidation phase identification

CMF (Chaikin Money Flow)

Measures buying/selling pressure through volume-weighted accumulation/distribution.

  • Accumulation/Distribution phases
  • ±0.05 strong pressure zones
  • Zero-line crossover signals

Elder Ray Index

Measures bull power (high - EMA13) and bear power (low - EMA13) for market strength.

  • Bull/Bear power analysis
  • Trend confirmation signals
  • Market dominance detection
